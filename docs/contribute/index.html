How to contribute

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Standard Notes. We are very happy that you want to help. The development for all our apps are done publicly on GitHub. Here are some ways that you can help us improve Standard Notes:

  1. Submit suggestions and feature ideas to our community forum. You can comment on the existing feature requests and subscribe to individual threads to follow their progress.
  2. Report bugs or issues to our community forum.
  3. Help others on the forum and our Slack group.
  4. Blog, tweet and share what you like about Standard Notes, what you use it for, and how it helps you.
